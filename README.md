# Untethered

**The honest income coach for people who are already qualified for better-paid remote work — and don't know it.**

A browser extension + companion app that scores any remote job for *real fit*, *real pay*, and
*real-remote / not-a-scam* as you browse — then walks you up an honest, month-by-month staircase to
$30/hr and beyond. V1 beachhead: **healthcare revenue-cycle (RCM) and patient-access roles.**

The one sentence no incumbent can answer for our user:

> *"Show me only fully-remote roles in my field that pay at least $30/hr, confirmed by the employer,
> are genuinely open, and aren't scams."*

## Why it's different

- **Employer-confirmed, sortable $/hr** — not the fabricated estimates Indeed/ZipRecruiter inject.
- **Trust signals** (real / really-remote / not-scam) for a user whose niche is the scam epicenter.
- **An honest fit checklist** — not an opaque, inflated 0–100 score.
- **The Income Staircase** — when you're not at $30 yet, it coaches you up the ladder instead of
  leaving you to guess. Realistic ranges, no guarantees, **no ISAs** — we never bet against your
  paycheck.
